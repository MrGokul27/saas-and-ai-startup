FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to know how to code?+
Not at all. Our workflow builder is fully visual and accepts natural language inputs. Most users run their first automation within 5 minutes of signing up.
How does the 14-day trial work?+
Your trial gives you full Pro-tier access for 14 days — no credit card needed. If you decide to continue, you'll be prompted to add payment. If not, your account downgrades to the free Starter plan.
Can I bring my own AI models?+
Yes. Pro and Enterprise plans let you connect external model endpoints (OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral, or self-hosted) via our unified model gateway. You can also fine-tune models directly within the platform.
Is my data safe and private?+
We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never train our models on your proprietary data.
What integrations are available?+
200+ native integrations including Slack, GitHub, Postgres, AWS, Stripe, HubSpot, Salesforce, and more. We also expose a full REST and webhook API for any custom system.
Can I self-host Stackly?+
Self-hosted and private cloud deployments are available on Enterprise plans. We provide Docker images, Kubernetes helm charts, and a dedicated engineering contact.
