FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

How can AI benefit my business?+
AI automates repetitive tasks, surfaces hidden data insights, and allows your team to focus on high-level creative strategy instead of manual processing.
Do I need coding skills to use Stackly?+
No coding required. Our visual builder and natural language engine allow anyone to deploy agents in minutes.
What kind of data does Stackly process?+
Stackly is designed to process a wide range of data types, including structured data from databases, unstructured text from documents and emails, and real-time streams from various SaaS applications. Our platform is flexible to integrate with your specific data ecosystem.
How does Stackly ensure data privacy and security?+
We prioritize data privacy and security with end-to-end encryption, strict access controls, and regular security audits. Stackly is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant, ensuring your data is handled with the highest standards.
Can I integrate Stackly with my existing tools?+
Absolutely. Stackly offers over 200 native integrations with popular business tools, databases, and cloud services. For custom solutions, our robust API and webhook support allow for seamless connectivity with virtually any system.
